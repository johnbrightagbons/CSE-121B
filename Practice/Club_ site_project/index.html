    • About Us

    Welcome to Sokponba Football Club, the home of passionate football players and enthusiastic supporters. We are dedicated to promoting the love of the beautiful game and fostering a sense of unity within our community.

    Our History

    Established in 2018, Sokponba FC has a rich history of success and commitment to excellence. Over the years, our club has grown into one of the most respected and competitive football clubs in the region.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to provide a platform for talented players to showcase their skills, develop their potential, and pursue their passion for football. We strive to create a supportive and inclusive environment where players can thrive and reach their full potential both on and off the field.

    Our Values

    • Passion: We are driven by our love for the game and our desire to create memorable football experiences.
    • Teamwork: We believe in the power of teamwork and collaboration to achieve our goals.
    • Respect: We treat everyone with respect, including our players, coaches, supporters, and opponents.
    • Fair Play: We uphold the principles of fair play, sportsmanship, and ethical conduct both on and off the pitch.
    • Community: We actively engage with our local community and strive to make a positive impact through football.

    Join Us

    If you share our passion for football, we welcome you to join Sokponba FC. Whether you are a player, coach, supporter, or volunteer, there are opportunities for you to get involved and be part of our football family.
